# HiddenStay — CEO Lounge One-Pager

**Group 63 I · Southeast Asia hidden homestays · SGD · Jul 2026**

---

## One line

**HiddenStay** = **5% booking marketplace** for independent homestays + **AI trip planner** that converts planning into direct bookings — hosts keep **95%**, guests plan and book in one place.

---

## Problem → Solution

| | Today (OTAs) | HiddenStay |
|---|--------------|------------|
| Host fee on SGD 100 stay | **SGD 18–30** (15–30%) | **SGD 5** (5%) |
| Host keeps | SGD 70–85 | **SGD 95** |
| Guest experience | Search elsewhere, book elsewhere | **Plan → map → book** same session |
| List property | Free | **Free** (host portal) |

---

## Product (capstone MVP)

- **Guests:** Search, Hidden Gems, AI planner, map itinerary, checkout  
- **Hosts:** Free portal — list, calendar, bookings, earnings, payouts  
- **Monetization in code:** 5% commission (`COMMISSION_RATE = 0.05`)

---

## How we earn (transparent — four streams)

| Stream | Price | Required? |
|--------|-------|-----------|
| Booking fee | **5%** | Yes |
| Growth tools (SEO, analytics) | **SGD 50/mo** | Optional — after **60-day ROI proof** |
| Featured listing | **SGD 99/mo** | Optional — when we have guest traffic |
| Performance ads | **7–10%** on **our** bookings only | Optional — **15% total cap** |

**Honest:** 5% alone ≈ **SGD 4.50** net per SGD 100 stay — **not enough** without volume + upgrades.

## Real-world fixes (not just a school project)

1. **3 host case studies by Month 6** before charging SGD 50/mo  
2. **15% fee cap** — ads only on bookings we drive  
3. **Guest plan:** AI planner (35%) + SEO (25%) + host referrals (20%) → **92 bookings/mo by Month 18**
