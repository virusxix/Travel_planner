## 2. Revenue model (transparent host pricing)

> **Trust principle:** **5% is honest only if Starter includes everything needed to list, book, and get paid.** Monthly fees are **optional upgrades** — never bait-and-switch.

### 2.0 Host portal vs Growth tools (naming)

| Term | What it is | Pay? |
|------|------------|------|
| **Host portal** | Login area — add listings, bookings, calendar, earnings, payouts | **Free** (every host uses this) |
| **Growth tools** | Optional upgrade — advanced SEO, analytics, extra photos | **SGD 50/mo** |

> **Host-facing line:** “Listing and managing your property is free — you only pay 5% when guests book. SGD 50/mo is only if you want advanced SEO and analytics.”

### 2.1 Four streams (always disclose together)

| Stream | Price | Required? | What it is |
|--------|-------|-----------|------------|
| **① Booking fee** | **5%** of guest total | **Yes** (all tiers) | Per completed booking — host keeps 95% |
| **② Growth tools** | **SGD 50/mo** or **SGD 500/yr** | **Optional** | Advanced SEO, analytics, extra photos — not basic host portal |
| **③ Featured listing** | **SGD 99/mo** per property | **Optional** | Homepage / landing-page spotlight — not required to list |
| **④ Performance advertising** | **7–10%** on attributed bookings | **Optional** | Promoted placement — **15% total cap** |

**Never market “only 5%” without showing this table on the same screen.**

### 2.1b Performance advertising tiers

**Attributed bookings only** (homepage, promoted search, SEO city page, AI planner). Direct/repeat = 5% only. **Hard cap: 15% total per booking.**

| Bookings that month | Ad fee (attributed) | Max total |
|---------------------|---------------------|-----------|
| **0–9** | **SGD 0** | 5% |
| **10–14** | **7%** | **12%** |
| **15+** | **10%** | **15%** |

*Full real-world playbook: **`HIDDENSTAY_MONEY_GUIDE.md`** §7–8.*

### 2.2 Host tiers — what’s included

| | **Starter** | **+ Growth tools** | **+ Featured** | **+ Perf. ads** |
|---|-------------|-------------------|----------------|-----------------|
| **Monthly fee** | **SGD 0** | **+SGD 50** | **+SGD 99** | **variable** |
| **Booking fee** | 5% | 5% | 5% | 5% + ad % when active |
| Host portal (list, book, payout) | ✓ | ✓ | ✓ | ✓ |
| Basic earnings & calendar | ✓ | ✓ | ✓ | ✓ |
| Advanced SEO & analytics | — | ✓ | ✓ | ✓ |
| Homepage / landing featured | — | — | ✓ | ✓ |
| Promoted SEO placement | — | — | — | ✓ (when busy) |
| **Typical max monthly** | **5% only** | **SGD 50 + 5%** | **+SGD 99** | **up to 15% cap** |

**Rule:** Do **not** paywall “see my bookings” or “get paid” behind Growth tools. That is what makes 5% feel like a scam.

### 2.3 Trust policies (slow months & upgrades)

| Policy | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| **60-day Growth tools trial** | First 20 hosts — try SEO tools before paying |
| **Pause Growth tools** | Host may pause SGD 50/mo after **30 days with zero bookings** |
| **No auto-upgrade** | Starter stays Starter until host clicks Upgrade |
| **Annual option** | SGD 500/yr — one clear number, ~2 months free |
| **Ads always opt-in** | Performance ads off by default; **SGD 0 ad fee** if &lt;10 bookings/month |
| **Fee schedule in ToS** | One page: booking %, subscriptions, ads, cancellation — signed at onboarding |

### 2.4 Host-facing copy (use on site, deck, outreach)

**Headline:**  
> **From 5% per booking. No monthly fee on Starter.**

**Subhead:**  
> List, accept bookings, and get paid on **Starter** — free host portal, you only pay when guests book. **Growth tools** (SGD 50/mo) add SEO and analytics. **Featured** (SGD 99/mo) and **performance ads** are optional. Guest room prices never increase because of our fees.

**Short pitch (WhatsApp / host fair):**  
> Booking.com took **SGD 18** on your last **SGD 100** stay. HiddenStay Starter takes **SGD 5** — same guest price, you keep **SGD 95**. Want SEO tools? Growth is **SGD 50/mo**, optional. We show your full cost next to your OTA invoice before you sign up.

**What we don’t say:**  
> ~~“Only 5%!”~~ · ~~“Cheapest platform!”~~ · ~~“No hidden fees!”~~ (without showing the tier table)

## 3. Real costs (Singapore stack)

### 3.1 Payment — Stripe Connect (Singapore)

When you run a marketplace with **your own pricing** (you collect 5%, pay host):

| Fee | Amount | Who pays (recommended) |
|-----|--------|------------------------|
| Card processing | **3.4% + SGD 0.50** per charge | **Host** (deducted from payout) |
| Connect active account | **SGD 2/month** per host with payout | Platform (cost of doing business) |
| Connect payout | **0.25% + SGD 0.50** per payout to host | Platform or host — model as **~SGD 0.80/booking** platform cost |
| Platform application fee | From **0.25%** of volume | Usually covered inside your 5% |

**Real net per SGD 100 booking (host pays card fees):**

```
Guest pays                         SGD 100.00
HiddenStay commission (5%)         SGD   5.00
Connect/payout platform cost       SGD  -0.80
AI/maps marginal cost              SGD  -0.30
────────────────────────────────────────────
Net to HiddenStay                  SGD   3.90
```

Use **SGD 4 net per booking** in conservative models.

## 6. Real-world strategy — phased playbook

### Phase A — Capstone (Months 1–4): prove product, not profit

**Goal:** 5 live pilots, Stripe sandbox → live, 30+ completed bookings total.

| Action | Cost | Revenue |
|--------|------|---------|
| Free Growth tools trial for all pilots | SGD 0 | Build case studies |
| Founder host visits (1 trip) | ~SGD 400 | Trust |
| No paid guest ads | SGD 0 | |
| **Target burn** | **~SGD 280/mo** | **~SGD 0–200/mo** early commission |

**KPI gate to Phase B:** 5 hosts, NPS ≥ 4/5, booking flow works, 2 hosts agree to paid plan after trial.

### Phase B — Single-city pilot (Months 5–12): prove economics

**City pick:** **Chiang Mai** or **Da Nang** — ADR ~SGD 53–58, homestay culture, low CAC via host associations.

| Action | Why |
|--------|-----|
| Convert pilots to **SGD 50/mo Growth tools** | Sub revenue before scale |
| Launch **Featured SGD 99/mo** + **performance ads** for 3 hosts max | Test ROI; ads opt-in only |
| SEO content: “homestay Chiang Mai book direct” | Guest acquisition without ads |
| Host pitch: side-by-side OTA invoice vs HiddenStay | Closes B2B faster than features |
| **Do not** open second city until **40 listings** in city 1 | Density > breadth |

**KPI gate to Phase C:** 50 listings, 100 bookings/month, MRR ≥ SGD 1,500, churn < 10%/quarter on paid plans.

### Phase C — Year 2: regional micro-brand

| Action | Target |
|--------|--------|
| Second city (same country first) | +30 listings |
| Part-time ops hire | When MRR > SGD 4,000 for 3 months |
| Apply Startup SG / university incubator | SGD 10–50k non-dilutive |
| Optional **annual Growth tools push** (SGD 500/yr) | Improves cash upfront |
| Guest referral: SGD 5 credit | Lower CAC than Meta ads |

## 10. Decision rules (use every month)

```
IF cash < SGD 8,000:
  → cut marketing to SGD 0, freeze promoted discounts, founders only

IF paid host conversion < 25% after 20 hosts:
  → extend Growth tools trial OR bundle 3 mo free — test 30 days

IF bookings/month > 100 AND MRR > SGD 1,500:
  → OK to spend up to SGD 500/mo on guest ads

IF commission net per booking < SGD 3 (after Stripe):
  → review payout structure; ensure host pays card fees

IF one city hits 50 listings:
  → only then expand geographically
```
