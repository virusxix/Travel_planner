## 1. The big picture (60 seconds)

**HiddenStay** is a booking platform for small, independent homestays in Southeast Asia — not luxury hotels.

**The problem:** Booking.com takes **15–30%** from hosts. We take **5%**.

**The catch:** 5% on a SGD 100 stay is only **~SGD 5**. That alone cannot pay our server bills.

**The solution:** Four income streams — only the first is required:

| # | Stream | Host pays? |
|---|--------|------------|
| 1 | **5% per booking** | Yes — always |
| 2 | **Growth tools** (SGD 50/mo) | Optional — after we **prove** SEO ROI |
| 3 | **Featured listing** (SGD 99/mo) | Optional — only when we have **guest traffic** |
| 4 | **Performance ads** (7–10% on **our** bookings only) | Optional — **capped at 15% total** |

**Starting cash:** SGD 15,000 · **Founder salaries:** SGD 0 until 3 profitable months in a row

**Main goal:** Break even at **Month 18**, profit from **Month 19+**, business **continues after capstone**.

### School project vs real business

| | School project only | **Real business (this guide)** |
|---|---------------------|--------------------------------|
| Success = | Demo works, judges happy | **Paying hosts + real bookings month after month** |
| After submission | Project ends | **Ops handbook, grant, 1 founder continues** |
| Guest plan | Assumed | **Written acquisition plan (§8)** |
| Host upgrades | Assumed 25% convert | **Prove ROI first (§7 Fix 1)** |
| Ad fees | Up to 25% total | **Capped at 15% (§7 Fix 2)** |

## 2. Who pays what?

### Guests
- Pay the **room price** set by the host (e.g. SGD 50/night × 2 nights = SGD 100).
- We do **not** add a guest service fee on top.

### Hosts
- **Must** use our website to list — that means logging into the **host portal** (free).
- Pay **5%** only when a guest actually books.
- Can optionally pay for extra tools and advertising (see §3).

### HiddenStay (us)
- Pays for servers, AI, maps, marketing, legal.
- Earns from the four streams above.

### Important naming (stops confusion)

| Term | Plain meaning | Monthly fee? |
|------|---------------|--------------|
| **Host portal** | Where hosts add listings, see bookings, get paid | **Free** |
| **Growth tools** | Extra SEO, analytics, more photos | **SGD 50** (optional) |

> **Say this to hosts:** “Listing and managing your property is free. You only pay 5% when guests book. SGD 50/month is only if you want advanced SEO tools — and we show you the numbers first.”

## 3. Our four ways to make money

### ① Booking fee — 5% (required)

- Taken from every completed stay.
- Host keeps **95%**.
- Small per booking — we need **volume**.

### ② Growth tools — SGD 50/month (optional)

- Advanced SEO, analytics, extra photos.
- **Not** required to list or get paid.
- Sold **only after** a 60-day trial + published case study (see §7).

### ③ Featured listing — SGD 99/month (optional)

- Spotlight on homepage / landing page.
- **Sell only from Month 10+** when we have real guest traffic — not before.

### ④ Performance advertising — capped, fair (optional)

**Real-world rule:** We only charge ad fees on bookings **we helped create** — not the host’s direct repeats or walk-ins.

| What counts as “our booking” | Examples |
|------------------------------|----------|
| ✅ Attributed to HiddenStay | Guest clicked from our homepage feature, promoted search, SEO city page, or AI planner → book |
| ❌ Not charged ad fee | Direct link, repeat guest, host’s own WhatsApp, walk-in |

**Ad fee tiers** (on **attributed** booking value only):

| Host’s total bookings that month | Ad fee on **our** bookings | **Max total we ever take** |
|----------------------------------|----------------------------|----------------------------|
| 0–9 | **SGD 0** | 5% (commission only) |
| 10–14 | **7%** on attributed bookings | **12%** per booking (5% + 7%) |
| 15+ | **10%** on attributed bookings | **15%** per booking (5% + 10%) — **hard cap** |

**Why the cap matters:** Old model could hit **25%** (5% + 20%) — same as Booking.com. Busy hosts would leave. **15% max** keeps us cheaper than OTAs even when advertising works.

**Example:** 20 bookings/month, SGD 120 avg, **8 attributed** to us:

```
Commission (all 20)     20 × SGD 120 × 5%     = SGD 120
Ad fee (8 attributed)    8 × SGD 120 × 10%    = SGD  96
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────
HiddenStay total                               = SGD 216
Host keeps on our 8 bookings                   = 95% − 10% ad = 85% of those stays
```

vs Booking.com at 18% on all 20: host loses **SGD 432** to the platform.

**Trust rules:**
- 60-day free Growth tools trial (first 20 hosts)
- Pause Growth tools after 30 days with zero bookings
- No auto-upgrade
- Never paywall “see my bookings” or “get paid”
- **2-night minimum stay** on new listings (protects tiny SGD 30 one-night margins)

## 4. One booking — worked example

**Typical stay:** SGD 50/night × 2 nights = **SGD 100**

```
Guest pays                           SGD 100.00
──────────────────────────────────────────────
Host gets (95%)                      SGD  95.00
HiddenStay gets (5%)                 SGD   5.00
  minus small API / ops cost         SGD   0.50
──────────────────────────────────────────────
What HiddenStay really keeps         SGD   4.50
```

**Same stay on Booking.com (~18%):** platform takes **SGD 18**, host keeps **SGD 82**.

### One host, 15 bookings/month

| | Amount |
|---|---|
| HiddenStay net (Starter only) | **SGD 67.50** |
| Our monthly bills (average) | **~SGD 900** |
| **Shortfall** | **-SGD 832.50** |

**~200 bookings/month** (whole platform) needed on commission alone. That is why we need **many hosts**, **guest demand**, and **some paid upgrades**.

## 5. Why the free tier exists (and its risk)

### Why Starter is free
- Hosts won’t join if they pay upfront before seeing guests.
- OTAs list for free — we must too.
- Supply first → bookings → revenue.

### The risk: everyone stays on Starter

| Scenario | Year 1 result | Cash left |
|----------|---------------|-----------|
| Normal bookings, no upgrades | **Loss ~SGD 4,650** | ~SGD 10,350 |
| Half the bookings | **Loss ~SGD 6,425** | ~SGD 8,575 |

**Fix:** Prove Growth tools work (§7) before expecting paid conversion. Target **~25%** on Growth tools by Month 18 — not 100%.

## 7. Three fixes that make this real-world viable

These are the difference between a **slide deck** and a **business that survives**.

### Fix 1 — Prove Growth tools ROI before Month 6

**Problem:** Rural hosts won’t pay SGD 50/mo on faith.

**What we do:**

| When | Action | Success metric |
|------|--------|----------------|
| Month 1–3 | Free Growth tools for 5 pilots | Baseline: listing views, search rank, enquiries |
| Month 4–5 | Measure before/after | **+30% listing views** or **+2 bookings** for 2+ hosts |
| Month 6 | Publish **3 case studies** (PDF + screenshot) | Host can see proof before paying |
| Month 7+ | Ask for SGD 50/mo | Target **15%** paid conversion by M12 → **25%** by M18 |

**Case study template:**

```
Host: [Name], Chiang Mai homestay
Before: 45 listing views/week, 2 bookings/month
After 60 days Growth tools: 78 views/week, 5 bookings/month
Cost: SGD 50/mo vs Booking.com saving ~SGD 87/mo on same volume
```

**If ROI not proven by Month 6:** extend trial 90 days — do **not** paywall the host portal.

---

### Fix 2 — Cap performance ads at 15% total

**Problem:** 5% + 20% ad fee = **25%** — we become the OTAs we criticize.

**What we do:**
- Ad fee **only on bookings we attribute** to our promotion
- **7–10%** ad rate (not 15–20%)
- **Hard cap: 15% total** per booking (5% commission + 10% ad max)
- **SGD 0 ad fee** if &lt;10 bookings that month

**Trade-off:** We earn **less per busy host** but **hosts stay** and **supply grows** — correct for Year 1–2.

---

### Fix 3 — Guest acquisition plan (not just hosts)

**Problem:** 55 hosts with no guests = everyone leaves.

**Target:** **92 bookings/month** by Month 18 (~1.7 bookings per listing).

| Channel | What we do | Cost | Expected share of bookings |
|---------|------------|------|--------------------------|
| **AI trip planner** | Guest plans 3-day route → books stays on map | In product (OpenAI) | **35%** |
| **SEO city pages** | “Hidden homestays Chiang Mai”, sample itineraries | Founder time | **25%** |
| **Host referrals** | Host shares listing link; guest books direct (5% only, no ad fee) | SGD 0 | **20%** |
| **Social proof** | 30s plan→book demo on Instagram/TikTok | &lt; SGD 100/mo | **10%** |
| **Pilot / university network** | Early adopters, UAT travellers | SGD 0 | **10%** |

**Guest CAC targets:**

| Phase | Max cost to acquire one booking |
|-------|--------------------------------|
| Months 1–12 | **&lt; SGD 15** (mostly organic) |
| Months 13–18 | **&lt; SGD 25** |
| Year 2+ (paid ads OK) | **&lt; SGD 40** |

**Month-by-month guest booking targets:**

| Month | Bookings | How we get there |
|-------|----------|------------------|
| 6 | 18 | Pilots + friends/family |
| 12 | 55 | Planner live + 5 SEO pages + 32 listings |
| 18 | 92 | 55 listings × 1.7 bookings + planner repeat use |

**Rule:** Do **not** spend &gt; SGD 300/month on paid guest ads until **50+ bookings/month** organic proves demand.

## 8. How we get guests (not just hosts)

A marketplace dies without **both sides**. This is our guest playbook.

### The hook: plan → map → book

1. Guest searches “3 days in Chiang Mai off the beaten path”
2. AI planner builds itinerary with **real bookable stays**
3. Guest books **inside the same session** — no redirect to Booking.com

**Why it matters:** We are not just a listing site — we **create demand** the OTAs do not offer.

### Content we publish (Months 4–12)

| Asset | Example | Purpose |
|-------|---------|---------|
| City guide + stays | “5 hidden homestays near Chiang Mai old town” | Google SEO |
| Sample itinerary | “Weekend: village → lodge → night market” | Shareable, shows planner |
| Host story | Photo + quote from pilot host | Trust |
| Comparison post | “What host keeps: Booking.com vs HiddenStay” | B2B + B2C |

### Repeat bookings

- Email/booking confirmation links back to planner for **next trip**
- Saved trips in guest dashboard
- **Repeat guests = 5% only, no ad fee** (they are not “attributed” promotion)

## 13. Weak points we accept (honest)

| Weak point | Why it hurts | How we address it |
|------------|--------------|-------------------|
| 5% is tiny per booking | Need ~200 bookings/mo commission-only | Four streams + volume (§8) |
| Hosts may not upgrade | SGD 50/mo is real money for village hosts | **Prove ROI first** (Fix 1) |
| Discovery vs Airbnb | Guests already know big brands | **Planner → book** loop (Fix 3) |
| Conservative year loses money | Cash drops to ~SGD 6.9k | Lean opex, grant, no salaries |
| Thailand/Vietnam payouts | Stripe friction | Start with supported payout countries; manual pilot fallback |
| Featured SGD 99 early | No traffic yet | **Defer to Month 10+** |
| Team graduates | Project dies | §11 continuation plan |

**Verdict:** **Survivable as a real pilot** on SGD 15k for 18 months **if Fix 1–3 execute**. **Not yet proven** as a large scalable company — that needs 100+ bookings/mo and post-capstone continuity.

## 14. What could go wrong

| Problem | What we do |
|---------|------------|
| Every host stays on Starter | Case studies by M6; extend trial; need 8+ paying hosts minimum |
| No guest bookings | Double down on planner SEO; do not add more hosts until guests come |
| Busy hosts reject ad fees | **15% cap** + attributed-only — show math vs OTA |
| AI costs spike | Cache itineraries; rate-limit free planner |
| Cash &lt; SGD 10k | Marketing zero; grant application |
| Capstone ends | Ops handbook + 1 founder + incubator |

**We never:** paywall listing/payouts · exceed **15% total** on a booking · open city 2 early · raise commission above 5% in panic.

## Quick math

```
Commission       = All bookings × ~SGD 120 × 5%
Growth tools     = Paying hosts × SGD 50/mo
Featured         = Featured properties × SGD 99/mo  (from M10+ only)
Perf. ads        = Attributed bookings × ~SGD 120 × 7–10%  (SGD 0 if <10 bookings/mo)
                   HARD CAP: 5% + ad fee ≤ 15% per booking
Total in         = Commission + Growth + Featured + Perf. ads
Profit           = Total in − Running costs
```
