# HiddenStay — How We Pay the Bills in the Early Years

> **What this document is:** A simple money plan for Year 1 and Year 2 — what we spend, what we earn, and how we stay alive before the business gets big.  
> **Easy-to-read version (start here):** **`HIDDENSTAY_MONEY_GUIDE.md`** — plain English, real-world survival fixes, guest acquisition, post-capstone plan.  
> **For market benchmarks, Stripe costs, three scenarios, and phased strategy:** see **`REAL_WORLD_FINANCIAL_STRATEGY.md`**
> **All amounts in:** Singapore dollars (SGD)  
> **How we make money:** **Four streams** — (1) **5% booking fee** (all tiers), (2) **optional Growth tools** (SGD 50/mo), (3) **optional Featured listing** (SGD 99/mo), (4) **optional performance advertising** (% of monthly bookings). **Starter is SGD 0/mo** — free **host portal** to list and get paid. See **`REAL_WORLD_FINANCIAL_STRATEGY.md` §2** for transparent host pricing & copy.
> **Important:** These are **estimates** for our capstone project — not official audited numbers.

## 1. The basics we assumed

Before the numbers, here is what we built the plan on:

| What we assumed | Amount | Why |
|-----------------|--------|-----|
| **Realistic hidden-stay price** | **SGD 40–65 per night** | Small homestays, eco-lodges, family-run guesthouses — not famous brands, often *cheaper* than chain hotels on big OTAs |
| **Typical stay length** | **2–3 nights** | Weekends, short trips — our AI planner pushes multi-night routes, not one-night hops |
| **Average booking (total trip)** | **SGD 120** | Example: SGD 50/night × 2 nights + small fees ≈ SGD 100–150 |
| Our cut (booking fee) | **5%** | All tiers — host keeps 95% |
| **Starter — host portal (free)** | **SGD 0/mo** | List, book, payout, earnings & calendar — **required to use the site, no monthly fee** |
| **Growth tools (optional)** | **SGD 50/mo or SGD 500/yr** | Advanced SEO, analytics, extra photos — **not** required to list or get paid |
| **Featured listing (optional)** | **SGD 99/mo per property** | Homepage / landing-page spotlight — **not** required to appear on the site |
| **Performance ads (optional)** | **7–10% on attributed bookings** (15% total cap) | Promoted placement — opt-in; SGD 0 if &lt;10 bookings/mo |
| **What we earn per booking (commission only)** | **~SGD 6.00** | 5% of SGD 120 — *plus* subscription & ad revenue from hosts |
| Team salaries in Year 1 | **SGD 0** | Student capstone — we work for free |
| Money in the bank at the start | **SGD 15,000** | Team savings + possible school grant |

**Important:** **5% is the booking fee on every tier.** The **host portal** (list, bookings, payouts) is **free** — hosts must log in to manage listings, but they never pay just to exist on the platform. We never paywall payouts or basic bookings behind Growth tools — that would break host trust.

*Pricing aligned with **`REAL_WORLD_FINANCIAL_STRATEGY.md` §2** (transparent tiers + host copy).*

## 3. One booking — where the commission goes

When a guest books a **typical hidden stay** — SGD 50/night for 2 nights (**SGD 100 total**):

```
Guest pays                           SGD 100.00
──────────────────────────────────────────────
Host gets (95%)                      SGD  95.00
HiddenStay gets (5%)                 SGD   5.00
  minus small API / ops cost         SGD   0.50
──────────────────────────────────────────────
What HiddenStay really keeps         SGD   4.50
```

Compare the **same stay on Booking.com** at 20% commission: the platform takes **SGD 20** — the host only gets **SGD 80**. HiddenStay is cheaper **for the host**, and the guest still pays **SGD 100** (same room, better economics for the owner).

**Break-even (commission only)** would need ~180 bookings/month — **too hard alone**. With **Growth tools + Featured + performance ads**, break-even drops to roughly **Month 5–6** (see §5).

## 8. Our survival plan — step by step

### Step 1 — Capstone (Months 1–3): spend almost nothing

- Use **test payments** only — no real Stripe fees yet  
- Free website tiers where possible  
- Limit AI usage so bills stay small  
- Get **5 pilot hosts** from people we know — no paid ads  
- **Nobody takes a salary**

**Cost:** ~**SGD 340/month** — at that rate, SGD 15k lasts **over 3 years**.

### Step 2 — Pilot (Months 4–9): prove it works

- Turn on **real payments** when we hit ~20 bookings/month  
- Marketing: social media, SEO, host referrals — **not** big ad spend yet  
- Offer **Growth tools (SGD 50/mo)** after 60-day trial — advanced SEO, optional  
- Offer **Featured listing (SGD 99/mo)** for hosts who want homepage spotlight — clearly labeled  
- Offer **performance ads** — opt-in only; **SGD 0 ad fee** when &lt;10 bookings that month  
- Goal: **combined revenue** covers costs by ~month 5, not commission alone

### Step 3 — Grow carefully (Months 10–18)

- Push **annual Growth tools (SGD 500/yr)** for upfront cash  
- Performance ads only where hosts see ROI (more enquiries)  
- Apply for **startup grants** if needed — buffer, not dependency

### Step 4 — Year 2+: small sustainable business

- Hire part-time help when we earn **SGD 5,000+ per month** steadily  
- Open a **second city** only after **50 active listings** in the first  
- Founders pay themselves only after **3 profitable months in a row**

## 12. What if the stay is only 1 night at SGD 30?

**Short answer:** At SGD 30 for one night, a pure 5% cut is **too small to live on**. We would **not** build the business around dorm beds alone — we use **rules and a mixed customer base** so cheap stays do not sink us.

### The math on one cheap night

Guest pays **SGD 30** for 1 night:

```
Guest pays                           SGD  30.00
──────────────────────────────────────────────
Host gets (95%)                      SGD  28.50
HiddenStay gets (5%)                 SGD   1.50
```

**SGD 1.50 per booking** is the problem. Payment systems (e.g. Stripe) charge about **2.9% + SGD 0.30** on the full amount:

```
Payment fee on SGD 30               ~SGD   1.17
```

| Who pays the payment fee? | What HiddenStay keeps |
|---------------------------|------------------------|
| **HiddenStay pays it** (bad) | **~SGD 0.33** — almost nothing |
| **Host pays it** from their 95% (better) | **~SGD 1.50** — still tiny |
| **Host pays it** + small API cost | **~SGD 1.00–1.30** |

### How many SGD 30 bookings to break even?

Monthly running costs ≈ **SGD 900**.

| We keep per booking | Bookings needed **every month** |
|---------------------|----------------------------------|
| SGD 0.33 (we pay Stripe) | **~2,700** — not realistic |
| SGD 1.50 (host pays Stripe) | **~600** — very hard early on |
| SGD 5 (SGD 120 total — old high-price plan) | **~75** — only if stays were much pricier |

**With optional Growth tools + performance ads:** Even when booking fee per stay is tiny, **SGD 50/mo Growth** plus optional **Featured / performance ads** is a **choice** — Starter hosts still pay **5% only** via the free host portal.

### How we still survive (four levers)

**1. We focus on 2–3 night trips, not one-night hops**  
Same SGD 50/night room: 1 night = SGD 50 total (we earn SGD 2.50). 3 nights = SGD 150 total (we earn **SGD 7.50**). The **AI trip planner** is not just a feature — it helps guests string villages and stays into a **longer route**, which is how we earn enough without raising nightly prices.

**2. Minimum booking rules (only if needed)**  
Examples we can add without changing the “5% for hosts” story:

| Rule | Example | Effect on SGD 30 × 1 night |
|------|---------|----------------------------|
| **Minimum stay** | 2 nights minimum | SGD 60 → commission **SGD 3.00** |
| **Minimum commission floor** | Charge **max(5%, SGD 5)** | We get **SGD 5.00**, not SGD 1.50 |
| **Minimum total booking** | No checkout under SGD 50 | Blocks worst-case one-nighters |

Many OTAs use a **floor fee** so tiny bookings do not lose money on payment costs.

**3. Payment fees stay with the host**  
Host receives 95% **after** payment processing — standard in travel. That keeps our **SGD 1.50** on a SGD 30 stay instead of **SGD 0.33**.

**4. Host upgrades + ads cover the gap**  
Even when booking fee on a cheap night is SGD 1.50, a host on **Growth tools (SGD 50/mo)** plus optional **Featured / performance ads** is a **choice** — Starter hosts still pay **5% only** through the free host portal.

**5. Multi-night stays**  
Even at SGD 40–65/night, a **weekend (2 nights)** or **long weekend (3 nights)** gives us SGD 4–10 per booking in commission — plus their subscription.

## 13. “Our stays are unknown — we can’t charge SGD 100+ per night”

**You’re right. And we don’t need to.**

### What we got wrong before

An earlier draft used **SGD 350 per booking** and talked about **SGD 115–175 per night**. That sounds like a boutique resort — **not** a hidden homestay in Chiang Rai or a family guesthouse in Da Nang.

### What hidden stays actually cost (Southeast Asia)

| Type of stay | Typical night rate | 2-night total | We earn (5%) |
|--------------|-------------------|---------------|--------------|
| Village homestay | SGD 35–45 | SGD 70–90 | SGD 3.50–4.50 |
| Small eco-lodge | SGD 50–65 | SGD 100–130 | SGD 5.00–6.50 |
| Independent guesthouse | SGD 55–75 | SGD 110–150 | SGD 5.50–7.50 |

These places are **less famous**, but that often means **more affordable for travellers** — not more expensive. HiddenStay wins by being **fair to hosts** (5% vs 15–30%), not by selling luxury.

### Why travellers still book unknown stays

| Reason | Example |
|--------|---------|
| **Cheaper** | SGD 50/night homestay vs SGD 120/night chain hotel nearby |
| **Authentic** | Cook with the family, not a hotel buffet |
| **Trip planner** | AI builds a 3-day route — the stay is part of a story, not a random room |
| **Trust** | Reviews, photos, clear host payout (95%) — community, not brand fame |

Guests are not paying for a **brand name**. They pay for **value + experience** — often **less** than a well-known hotel.

### How we survive without high nightly prices

| Lever | What it means |
|-------|----------------|
| **Growth tools (SGD 50/mo)** | Optional revenue **before** bookings scale — hosts choose to upgrade |
| **Featured + performance ads** | Optional — clearly labeled; **SGD 0 ad fee** in slow months |
| **Multi-night trips** | 3 nights × SGD 50 = SGD 150 → **SGD 7.50** commission per booking |

### Example: same guest, same budget

**Budget:** SGD 200 for accommodation on a 3-day trip.

| Option | Nightly | Nights | Total | Platform cut |
|--------|---------|--------|-------|--------------|
| Chain hotel on Booking.com | SGD 80 | 2 | SGD 160 | ~SGD 32 (20%) |
| Hidden homestay on HiddenStay | SGD 55 | 3 | SGD 165 | **SGD 8.25 (5%)** |

Guest gets **more nights** and a **local stay** for similar money. Host keeps **~SGD 157** instead of **~SGD 128**. We earn **SGD 8** — small, but **180 such bookings/month** covers our costs.

### What we tell judges

| Question | Answer |
|----------|--------|
| Are we a luxury platform? | **No** — hidden, affordable, independent stays. |
| Do we need SGD 100+/night? | **No** — we need **2–3 nights at SGD 40–65** and enough bookings. |
