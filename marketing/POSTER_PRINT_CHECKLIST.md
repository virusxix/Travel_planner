## JCU requirements (from guide)

| Rule | Spec |
|------|------|
| Paper | **A1** — 594 × 841 mm (23.4 × 33.1 in) |
| Orientation | **Vertical (portrait) only** |
| Margins | **~1.5 inches (38 mm)** — content inside safe area |
| Title | **60 pt** — centered between logos |
| Logos | **JCUS seal (left) + JCU Singapore (right) — do not move** |
| Resolution | Export **200–300 DPI** for print |

---

## Before you print

1. **Add official logos** — replace placeholder boxes in HTML with PNG paths:
   - Left: JCUS 18 seal  
   - Right: James Cook University Singapore logo  
2. Open `hiddenstay-capstone-poster.html` in **Chrome**
3. Print → **Save as PDF** (or use browser print to PDF at 100% scale)
4. Verify margins — nothing clipped at edges
5. Submit PDF at **200–300 DPI** (print shop can confirm)
